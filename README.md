# VoiceBase
A Unified Voice-First Open Source Knowledge Platform
Hello open-source community,

We are building VoiceBase — a collaborative platform designed to bring together open-source models, coding tools, and research from across the internet into one unified, voice-driven knowledge base.

The vision:

	•	A single platform where contributors worldwide share code, research, and tools
	•	Voice-first interface making knowledge accessible to everyone
	•	Community-driven, cradle to grave — built and maintained by the people who use it

What we need:

	•	Developers interested in voice interfaces and AI
	•	Researchers willing to contribute knowledge and papers
	•	Open-source advocates who believe in collective intelligence
	•	Anyone passionate about democratizing access to AI tools

How to contribute:

	•	Star and watch this repository
	•	Share with your network
	•	Open an issue introducing yourself and your skills

This is built on unity, strength, and knowledge. Let’s build it together.
